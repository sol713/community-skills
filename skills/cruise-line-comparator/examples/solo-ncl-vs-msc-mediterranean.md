# Solo Mediterranean Example

## User Prompt

I'm a solo traveler in my 30s looking at a 7-night Mediterranean cruise. I'm comparing Norwegian and MSC because both have decent-looking prices. I care about not paying a crazy solo supplement, meeting people, good port access, and not being surprised by fees. Which should I choose?

## Expected Output Shape

TOP RECOMMENDATION: Norwegian if the specific sailing has a reasonable solo fare or solo cabin and the onboard social setup matters

RUNNER-UP: MSC if the final solo price is meaningfully lower and the itinerary is stronger

AVOID / ONLY IF: Either line if the final solo price, service charges, transfers, or port logistics erase the base-fare savings

Confidence: Medium until exact solo quotes, ship, cabin type, and itinerary are provided

Why:
- Norwegian often has stronger solo-traveler positioning when the ship has solo cabins, solo lounge, or solo meetups.
- MSC may win on raw price, but the final solo fare and inclusions need careful checking.
- For the Mediterranean, port access and transfer costs can matter as much as ship amenities.

| Criteria | Norwegian | MSC |
|---|---|---|
| Best traveler fit | Solo traveler wanting social structure | Deal hunter comfortable with international atmosphere |
| Budget fit | Strong only if solo pricing is reasonable | Often aggressive base fares |
| Food and dining | Flexible dining can help solo travelers | Verify included dining expectations |
| Entertainment and activities | Stronger solo/social potential on the right ship | Varies by ship and passenger mix |
| Cabin/value notes | Look for solo cabin or low supplement | Watch cabin category and market-specific inclusions |
| Itinerary fit | Compare port times and embarkation city | Compare port times and transfer logistics |
| Hidden-cost risk | Promo/package terms and gratuities | Service charges, fare inclusions, drinks/Wi-Fi |

Fit / not-fit:
- Norwegian: Best if avoiding solo isolation matters; not ideal if the final solo price is far higher.
- MSC: Best if price and itinerary dominate; not ideal if you want a more structured solo social setup.

Hidden-cost watchouts:
- Solo supplement or solo cabin availability.
- Package gratuities, drinks, Wi-Fi, and dining terms.
- Mediterranean port transfers, especially where the port is far from the city center.

Caveats:
- I do not have live fares, cabin inventory, promo terms, or port transfer pricing here, so verify final checkout totals and itinerary logistics.
- The specific ship matters: do not assume every NCL or MSC sailing has the same solo experience.

CTA:
Score the exact ships, solo cabin prices, and port times side by side before booking.
