# Cruise Line Comparison Scoring Rubric

Use this scorecard when comparing cruise lines, ships, or routes. Scores are directional planning guidance unless the user provides final quotes and itinerary details.

## Default Weights

Use a 100-point total.

| Criterion | Default Weight | What To Evaluate |
|---|---:|---|
| Traveler fit | 20 | Family, solo, couple, luxury, first-timer, mobility, age mix |
| Budget fit | 20 | Final total, not just base fare; cabin compromise; included perks |
| Itinerary fit | 15 | Ports, sea days, destination depth, departure convenience |
| Ship and activity fit | 15 | Ship age/class, activities, entertainment, kids/teen programming |
| Food and dining fit | 10 | Included food, specialty temptation, flexible/traditional dining |
| Service and atmosphere | 10 | Casual vs premium, crowd tolerance, formality, nightlife |
| Hidden-cost risk | 10 | Drink/Wi-Fi/dining packages, gratuities, private-island extras, transfers |

## Priority Overrides

Adjust weights when the user gives top priorities:

- Kids/teens/activity priority: increase `Ship and activity fit` to 25.
- Deal-focused priority: increase `Budget fit` and `Hidden-cost risk` to a combined 40.
- Destination-first priority: increase `Itinerary fit` to 30.
- Luxury/service priority: increase `Service and atmosphere` to 20 and reduce activity weight.
- Solo priority: add solo supplement, solo cabins, social programming, and safety fit under `Traveler fit`.

## Score Labels

Use 1-5 scores per criterion:

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 5 | Excellent fit for this user's stated priorities |
| 4 | Strong fit with manageable tradeoffs |
| 3 | Usable, but not clearly better than alternatives |
| 2 | Noticeable mismatch or expensive compromise |
| 1 | Poor fit unless the price or itinerary is uniquely strong |

## Recommendation Thresholds

- Top recommendation: highest weighted score and no severe mismatch with the user's top priority.
- Runner-up: close score or better if prices/ship class shift.
- Avoid / only if: any option with a severe mismatch on the user's top priority, weak ship fit, poor itinerary fit, or high hidden-cost risk.

## Confidence

| Confidence | Use When |
|---|---|
| High | User supplied specific ships, dates, cabin type, quotes, and priorities |
| Medium | User supplied brands/route/priority but not exact ships or final quotes |
| Low | User asked broad brand questions without dates, ships, cabin, or budget |
