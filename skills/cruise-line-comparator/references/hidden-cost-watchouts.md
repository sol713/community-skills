# Hidden Cost Watchouts

Use this checklist to keep comparison answers focused on total trip cost, not base fare.

## Universal Cost Categories

- Taxes, port fees, and government fees.
- Daily gratuities or service charges.
- Drinks, soda, bottled water, premium coffee, and alcohol packages.
- Wi-Fi packages.
- Specialty dining and premium casual dining.
- Shore excursions and private-island activities.
- Photos, arcade, casino, spa, fitness classes, classes, and paid attractions.
- Transfers, parking, pre-cruise hotels, flights, luggage, and travel insurance.
- Cabin location tradeoffs, upgrade bids, refundable deposit differences, and fare class restrictions.

## Brand-Specific Watchouts

| Line | Watchouts |
|---|---|
| Carnival Cruise Line | Base fare can look strong, but add gratuities, Wi-Fi, drinks, specialty dining, shore excursions, and arcade/kids extras |
| Royal Caribbean International | Newer ships and private destinations can add premium activities, dining, drink packages, Wi-Fi, and cabana/day-pass temptations |
| Norwegian Cruise Line | Promo bundles may still carry gratuities, service charges, or fare differences; compare the final checkout total |
| MSC Cruises | Inclusions and service charges can vary by market and fare; verify dining, drinks, Wi-Fi, and Yacht Club inclusions |
| Disney Cruise Line | High base fares may include some family value, but specialty drinks, port adventures, Bibbidi Bobbidi Boutique, photos, and adult dining add up |
| Princess Cruises | Bundles like Plus/Premier can change the value math; compare bare fare vs bundled fare for the same cabin |
| Celebrity Cruises | Always compare fare type, included perks, drink/Wi-Fi bundles, and gratuities in final checkout |
| Holland America Line | Have It All-style bundles may be good value for some travelers; check whether the included perks match real usage |
| Virgin Voyages | Many inclusions are bundled differently than mainstream lines, but shore things, bar tab, spa, excursions, and premium extras still matter |

## Output Rule

Always flag at least three hidden-cost watchouts tied to the user's specific comparison. If the user has exact quotes, ask for whether taxes, fees, gratuities, perks, and onboard packages are included before ranking on price.
